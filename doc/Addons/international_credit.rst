
====================== 
International Credit
======================

.. image:: /Images/international_credit_logo.png
	:height: 100px
  

**Purpose**
 - Add an advanced level of control in making international calls by allocating designated balance to customers

This will be used for the:

 1) Prepaid customers
 2) Postpaid customers
 
 
**Stpes for install**

 - Click on the **Get Addons**.
 - Click on the **Community**.
 - Click on the **install** button from **International Credits**.

**Menu Location**

 Accounts -> International Credits
